It started with two moms and a problem.

A bunch of neighborhood families missed the summer-camp sign-ups. Suddenly the kids were home, bored, and bummed — and so were the parents.

So two moms made a plan: gather the kids, run a week-long camp right in the neighborhood. Each parent takes a few hours and picks the day — hiking, free play, swimming, learning about animals and trees, even setting up little lemonade-stand businesses. Older siblings and teens help lead.

Today was day one. Every single kid loved it.
